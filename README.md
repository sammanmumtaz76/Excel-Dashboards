# 📊 Excel Dashboards Portfolio

A collection of three Excel dashboards showcasing my skills in data cleaning, pivot table analysis, KPI tracking, and business intelligence reporting — built from raw datasets and transformed into insight-driven reports.

---

## 📁 Dashboards Included

### 1. Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard
Analyzes transactional retail data across U.S. states, product categories, and customers. Tracks revenue trends, profit margins, and product-level performance using pivot tables, conditional formatting, and dynamic charts.

### 2. Employee Productivity Dashboard
An HR analytics dashboard covering 50 employees across 6 departments. Monitors performance scores, attendance rates, salary benchmarks, training investment, and promotion eligibility to support data-driven workforce decisions.

### 3. Recruitment Funnel Dashboard
Maps the end-to-end hiring pipeline across 5 recruiters and 5 job roles — from application to final hire. Tracks stage-by-stage conversion rates to identify bottlenecks and measure recruiter effectiveness.
